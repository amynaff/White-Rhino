White Rhinoceros

Worlds Last Male White Rhino Dies!

  • Scientific Name: Ceratotherium simum
  • Height: 1.8 meters(6 feet)
  • Lifespan: 40-50 years
  • Weight: 2,000 kilos(2 tons)

The world has been captivated by the loss of a rhino named Sudan, who passed away at Ol Pejeta Conservancy in Kenya this week from age-related complications. Elderly for his species, the 45-year-old Sudan lived a long and generally good life that would be almost unthinkable in the wild. His death was not unexpected, yet it has resonated around the world. As the planet’s last male northern white rhinoceros, Sudan found stardom as the so-called “last man standing.” Some affectionately called him “the most eligible bachelor in the world.”
